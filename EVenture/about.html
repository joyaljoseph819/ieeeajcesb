What is Eventure?

EVenture is a 2-day technical conclave organized by the Industry Applications Society Chapter of IEEE SB AJCE. The conclave brings together electric vehicle enthusiasts, industry experts, and students to discuss and learn about the latest trends and innovations in the electric vehicle industry.

The event will feature a range of activities and programs designed to educate and engage attendees, including a panel discussion, EV Entrepreneurship Pitch Competition, Keynote Speech/EV Showcase, Treasure Hunt Game, Cultural Events, Network Session, Basic Workshop for School Students, JAM(Just a Minute), Workshop on EV Battery Design Using MATLAB and EVTechnologies.

We are conducting this event in an affordable manner for the participants so that everyone interested can be a part of EVenture.

Why Eventure?

EVenture is an opportunity for professionals, innovators, and enthusiasts to advance the EV industry and work towards a sustainable future. With the keynote speech, panel discussion, EV showcase, workshops, and networking sessions, attendees will gain knowledge, experience, and exposure to the latest developments and trends in the EV industry.

The event also helps to evangelize the benefits of being an IAS member. This also provides a professional development source for the students. It provides a platform to interact with professionals, students, and senior and professional members of IEEE.

About IEEE SB AJCE

Under the auspices of the Student Branch, the Computer Society, Women in Engineering affinity chapter, Solid State Circuits Society, SIGHT and Industrial Applications Society have been founded to help keep members technically up-to-date, network, and cooperate on research and projects. The AJCE Student Branch consists of 150 plus active IEEE Student and Professional Membership holders. It provides a supportive and stimulating environment for students to explore their interests, engage in hands-on learning experiences, and connect with the wider engineering community

EVenture is a two-day conclave focused on electric vehicles. It brings together enthusiasts, experts, and students to discuss industry trends. The event features panel discussions, workshops, exhibitions, and cultural activities for an informative and enjoyable experience.
