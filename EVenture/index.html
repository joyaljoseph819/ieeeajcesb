About The Event

EVenture is a three-day conclave focused on electric vehicles. It brings together enthusiasts, experts, and students to discuss industry trends. The event features panel discussions, workshops, exhibitions, and cultural activities for an informative and enjoyable experience.

IEEE SB AJCE

Under the auspices of the Student Branch, the Computer Society, Women in Engineering affinity chapter, Solid State Circuits Society, SIGHT and Industrial Applications Society have been founded to help keep members technically up-to-date, network, and co-operate on research and projects. The AJCE Student Branch consists of 150 plus active IEEE Student and Professional Membership holders. It provides a supportive and stimulating environment for students to explore their interests, engage in hands-on learning experiences, and connect with the wider engineering community.
